Charity

Mercury Phoenix Trust

The Mercury Phoenix Trust (charity no. 1013768) was founded by Brian May, Roger Taylor and their manager Jim Beach in memory of rock band Queen's iconic lead singer Freddie Mercury who died in 1991 from complications related to AIDS.

Cycling the Outer Hebrides

I cycled up the outer hebrides which was a total of 270 kilometres and 10 islands with a friend for this charity over the summer of 2017 over the course of 5 days. We started the year with not having cycled in 10 years and managed to do this for the charity above.

Practice

For me and my friends practice for cycling up the outer hebrides we cycled every saturday for half a year. The distance varied but we were out for at least 3 hours at a time no matter the weather. We set up a route that we normal went by the end of it
