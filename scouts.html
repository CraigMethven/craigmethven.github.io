Young Leader

Training

Made in my first year of university. A grid based game where players take it in turn to place counters on a board in a place which would make an opponants piece surrounded by at least two of your own. The opponants pieces in between these two are then changed to your counter. This game also has an AI that you can play against. The aim of the game is to have the most pieces on the board by the end of the game.
